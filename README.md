## 🎯 What Is TrustLens?

**TrustLens** is a free, privacy-first, browser-based misinformation risk analysis system designed to help users evaluate the *credibility, reliability, and risk signals* of online text content — **without tracking users or storing personal data**.

In an era where fake news, health scams, financial fraud, and political manipulation spread faster than fact-checking, TrustLens acts as your **first line of defense**. It doesn't determine absolute truth — instead, it analyzes *patterns, signals, and context* commonly associated with misinformation, manipulation, and misleading claims, giving you the confidence to pause before sharing.

Built by [LYF Mail](https://lyfmail.com), a [privacy-first digital wellness platform](https://lyfmail.com) serving 50,000+ subscribers across India since 2020, TrustLens extends our mission of **"Every Choice Shapes Tomorrow"** into digital literacy and media safety.

## 🚨 Why TrustLens Exists

The modern internet moves faster than fact-checking. Every day, millions of people are exposed to:

- 📰 **Sensational headlines** designed to trigger emotional reactions.
- 💊 **Miracle health claims** that can cause real physical harm.
- 💰 **Financial "guarantees"** and get-rich-quick scams targeting vulnerable victims.
- 📲 **Forwarded viral messages** that exploit trust in friends and family.
- 🏛️ **Politically charged narratives** engineered to spread fear and social division.
- ⏰ **Urgency traps** that bypass critical thinking with false time pressure.

**Most people don't share misinformation intentionally.** They share it because it sounds urgent, looks authoritative, or comes from someone they trust. TrustLens slows down that moment — the critical moment between receiving and forwarding — giving you the tools to **see clearly before you share**.

## 🧠 How TrustLens Works

TrustLens uses a **multi-layered signal detection system** to analyze content in real-time:

### 1. Content Classification
The system examines the structure and intent of the text to identify manipulation frameworks:
- **Sensational language detection** — flags exaggerated, emotionally charged phrasing.
- **Urgency pattern analysis** — identifies false time pressure ("Act now!", "Limited time!").
- **Forwarded message indicators** — detects chain-message patterns common in viral misinformation.
- **Source verification checks** — evaluates authority signals and attribution quality.

### 2. Claim & Risk Detection
Checks for known high-risk patterns and red flags across critical categories:
- **Fake news language patterns** — linguistic markers of fabricated stories.
- **Unverified health claims** — miracle cures, unproven treatments, and medical scams.
- **Political manipulation tactics** — engineered narratives designed to provoke division.
- **Financial scam indicators** — investment fraud, Ponzi schemes, and get-rich-quick traps.

### 3. Signal Aggregation & Scoring
Each detected signal contributes to an overall credibility assessment:
- **Weighted risk scoring** — different signals carry different risk weights.
- **Pattern correlation analysis** — multiple suspicious signals increase confidence.
- **Confidence level calculation** — transparent scoring from 0-100%.
- **Credibility indexing** — categorizes content as Low, Medium, or High risk.

### 4. Explainability & Transparency
Complete transparency in how every decision is made:
- **Triggered signal display** — shows exactly which patterns were detected.
- **Risk explanation breakdown** — human-readable reasoning for each flag.
- **Contextual guidance** — educational tips on why the pattern is suspicious.
- **Educational context** — helps users learn to spot misinformation independently.

## ⚠️ Important Limitations

TrustLens is a **first-line screening tool**, not a replacement for professional judgment. Please understand:

- **Not Real-Time Verification:** We cannot verify every claim against live databases in real-time.
- **Not Professional Fact-Checking:** We are a preliminary tool, not a replacement for professional fact-checkers like BOOM, Alt News, or AFP.
- **Not Absolute Truth:** We provide risk guidance and confidence scores, not definitive truth verdicts.
- **Pattern-Based Detection:** We detect manipulation patterns, not intent or nuanced context.
- **Continuously Improving:** Our signals evolve, but new manipulation techniques emerge constantly. Stay vigilant.

## 💬 Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is TrustLens free to use?**  
A: Yes. TrustLens is completely free to use. No subscription, no hidden fees, no data monetization.

**Q: Does TrustLens store my messages or personal data?**  
A: No. All analysis happens locally in your browser. We never see, store, or process your content on our servers.

**Q: Can TrustLens detect all fake news?**  
A: No system is perfect. TrustLens detects *manipulation patterns*, not absolute truth. It is a first-line tool, not a replacement for professional fact-checking.

**Q: Does TrustLens work with WhatsApp?**  
A: Yes. Simply copy any forwarded message from WhatsApp and paste it into TrustLens for instant analysis before sharing.

**Q: Is TrustLens politically biased?**  
A: No. We analyze linguistic and structural patterns associated with manipulation, not political content or ideology.

**Q: Can I install TrustLens as an app?**  
A: Yes. TrustLens is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Visit [trustlens.lyfmail.com](https://trustlens.lyfmail.com) on your mobile browser and tap "Add to Home Screen."

**Q: Does TrustLens work offline?**  
A: Partially. The core detection engine works offline after your first visit. Some advanced features may require connectivity.

**Q: How can I contribute to TrustLens?**  
A: While the core engine is proprietary, we welcome contributions to documentation, translations, educational resources, and community outreach. See [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](CONTRIBUTING.md).
